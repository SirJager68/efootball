How to Play

This online version is very similar to the original vibrating tabletop game, but with some modern twists.

Setup: Each player controls a team of miniature players on a virtual field. Players can select formations, plays, and control player movements using the mouse or keyboard. Once you set a play you can start the game by hitting the Space Bar.

Gameplay: - ENTER THE GAME - First player in is the Home team, second player is the Away team. - IMPORTANT - once in DO NOT REFRESH YOUR BROWSER. (working on this...) - Play will begin with kickoff (simulated now. kicking soon) - Choose your play from the playbook (offense or defense). - Use the directional controls to guide your players. - mouse over - highlight player and see their Card - left-click to move and rotate (mouse Wheel) player - press (x) plus mouse wheel to change player speed - press (i) to open the playbook and select play - once both players are set. press SPACE BAR to start play - DURING PLAY: - press (space) to stop play and adjust plays (RULES COMING) - press (p) to enable Pass modern - PASS modern - rotate any player not engaged with another player - right click on eligible receiver (highlighted players) - once receiver is selected, left click over QB and pull Pass Band back to throw the ball - play will immediately start Again - RULE ? - stop play after catch to all players to be adjusted. - TACKLE, Out of Bounds, Pass Incomplete - once tackle, out of bounds or pass incomplete occurs, the play stops and players can be adjusted. - press (r) to reset play and start again by selecting plays. IN DEVELOPMENT: -kicking and punting -set game parameters (clock duration, playclock, etc.) r - reset play i - open playbook p - enable pass mode space - start play x - change player speed (hold x and use mouse wheel to adjust speed) z - reset zoom a - center on hightlighted player q - restarts game + refresh browser

Winning: The game ends when time runs out or a player reaches a predetermined score. The player with the most points wins!

About the Electrik Football Project

Electrik Football is a modern, browser-based twist on classic tabletop electric football. Inspired by the tactile thrill of guiding miniature players on a vibrating metal field, this digital adaptation brings multiplayer action, live animations, and strategic play-calling to your screen.

We’ve built a full 3D‐inspired yard line system, nuanced playbooks for offense and defense, and real‐time websockets to let you challenge friends from anywhere. Whether you remember the original wooden “gridiron” or you’re discovering electric football for the first time, Electrik Football offers a nostalgic yet fresh gaming experience.

Key features include:

  • Live play selection overlay—pick from shotgun, wishbone, I‐formation, and more.
  • Animated player movements guided by dial values and headings.
  • Pass and kick modes for aerial strategy and special teams.
  • Multigame support—host or join multiple simultaneous matches.
  • Retro‐styled interface with neon grids and futuristic vibes.
